Notes on the eBook edition

This eBook edition was compiled and typeset by Bayard Randel. It was derived from the New Alexandria Archive digital edition pdf, which was scanned from the original copy no. 37, and hand corrected by Igor Dolgov, Zachary Jones, and Greg Golden.

Changes from the original text

In order to adapt this work to eBook formats, a number of changes have been made:

  1. While Mushrooms, Russia and History was originally published in two volumes, both volumes are presented here as a single eBook for convenience. References to volumes in the text have been removed.
  2. As eBook page layouts are fluid and device dependant, the position of figures and plates have been changed to improve clarity.
  3. Footnotes are rendered at the end of chapters and numbered per chapter rather than per page.
  4. References to page numbers from the original text have been removed. Links instead are provided from appendicies and indicies.
